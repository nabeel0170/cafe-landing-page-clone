Our Story
I am Jessie Abrahamson and I began my journey right here in Oak Harbor, WA, working alongside David and Nadya Flynn back in 1998. They were the proud owners of Gyros R Us Cafe, which later transformed into Flynn's Greek House, and eventually Nadya's Greek Cuisine before I took over the business. I accompanied them to countless festivals, immersing myself in the culinary world. In 2006, I made the pivotal decision to purchase my first food truck, a decision that has guided me ever since.
In late 2018, Nadya informed me of her decision to step back from the cafe business. We had a conversation about the possibility of me taking over, given my deep-rooted connection with the cafe since its early days. By January 2019, together with my partner Chris, we officially acquired the cafe where I had honed my skills over the years. We successfully transitioned the cafe's legacy from a humble food truck to a beloved establishment in Oak Harbor, and thus began the journey of our cafe, now known as 'Café du Monde'.
